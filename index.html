Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of paying your LIC premiums through net-banking/phone-banking?

Online payment is fast,secure and authentic. You get better services like policy tracking, dashboard etc. There are other benefits like avoiding expenses of postage/courier in sending the remittance to LIC, avoid queues for making payment and hence save time, you can decide in advance the date on which you want your account to be debited etc

Can I avail a loan against my LIC Policy? If yes, for how much amount?

Yes, you can avail a loan against your LIC policy provided you have paid premiums for 3 years. However, you must consider the following factors before applying for the loan:A loan can be obtained only after 3 years of the policy's initiation, Interest on a loan can be paid half yearly etc

How do I pay premium online for LIC?

Go to LIC’s official website and select Pay Premium Online option to pay the premium online.

How to download LIC’s premium payment receipt?

You can download the premium receipt online through LIC's e-services from LIC's official website.
